Our Team

Jon Meyers

I am a college senior studying marketing at the University at Buffalo. Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to work with a few startups, gaining hands-on experience in building and scaling businesses.

Since a young age, I have been deeply passionate about sustainability and making the world a better place. I believe I was put on this Earth to do something great, leaving behind a legacy that rivals the greats of history and drives substantial, positive change in the world.

While I may not be the best at any one thing, my strength lies in my problem-solving skills and work ethic, allowing me to figure out solutions to any challenge. This mindset defines my approach to building Atlas and tackling the complex issues we face today.

My vision for the team is to create an environment where everyone is excited about their work. Guided by the motto, "Work hard, play hard," I envision a team of passionate individuals who excel at what they do while continuously learning and expanding their capabilities. At the same time, we prioritize enjoying our free time and fostering a strong sense of camaraderie.

Looking ahead, my goal for Atlas is to push the boundaries of environmental sustainability by exploring new technologies. In addition, I aim to develop high-speed and maglev rail systems to transform transportation, revitalize the U.S. economy, and reconnect our cities and culture. On the creative side, I dream of establishing Pretzel Day Productions, an Atlas company dedicated to producing engaging TV shows and movies.

Beyond work, I enjoy hiking, running, fishing, boating, and traveling. I find the world to be a beautiful place, and I am committed to making it even better. While some billionaires are looking to other planets to solve humanity’s problems, I firmly believe that we can and must address our challenges here on Earth.
